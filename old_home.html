Who We Are

Founded in 2021, Journalist Welfare Trust brings together media professionals from diverse backgrounds with a shared commitment to truth, ethical reporting, and mutual support. We exist to serve and empower journalists through training, advocacy, resources, and a strong professional network.

Our Mission

Our mission is to champion the welfare of journalists while upholding the values of ethical journalism and press freedom. We are committed to:

  • Promoting journalistic integrity: Advocating for fairness, accuracy, and transparency in media.
  • Protecting press freedom: Supporting the rights of journalists and access to information.
  • Empowering the profession: Providing skills training, mentorship, and career development.
  • Fostering solidarity: Building a strong, supportive community for journalists at all stages of their careers.

Member Benefits

  • Access to exclusive training sessions and networking events

  • Use of our press lounge and professional meeting spaces

  • Eligibility for recognition and journalism awards

  • A trusted community of peers, mentors, and collaborators

  • Support in times of professional need or crisis
