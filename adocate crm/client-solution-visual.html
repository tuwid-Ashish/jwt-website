Central Platform for Legal Work, Filings & Transactions

A client-friendly visual summary for an advocate office that also handles government form filling, ITR, PAN, ration card, and similar service jobs. The platform brings client records, payments, portal fees, invoices, receipts, and team workflow into one management system.

01

One job card
Every client request is tracked as a single file with service, document, payment, and status history.

02

One ledger
Cash, UPI, bank payments, and office-paid fees are recorded against the same job for clarity.

03

One dashboard
Owner and manager can see collections, pending amounts, expenses, and staff activity in real time.

Solution Flow

1
Client IntakeCapture customer name, service type, contact details, and basic file information.
2
Job CreationCreate a digital job card with documents needed, fees, and assigned staff member.
3
Payment & ExpenseRecord advance or full payment, and log portal or office-paid charges.
4
Delivery & ReportsComplete the work, issue receipt or invoice, and track balance with reports.

Who Uses It

Owner / AdminControls service types, fee structure, reports, and all financial visibility.
ManagerAssigns work, checks pending files, and monitors team productivity.
OperatorCreates jobs, updates status, uploads documents, and records customer payment.
Billing StaffGenerates receipts, matches collections, and reconciles portal expenses.
