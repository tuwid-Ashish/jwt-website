Types of Events We Organize

🎪 Community Events

Networking meetups, community gatherings, and social events that bring journalists together to build professional relationships and foster solidarity within the profession.

🎓 Training Programs

Professional development workshops covering digital skills, fact-checking, legal awareness, safety training, data journalism, and career mentorship.

🏆 Sports Tournaments

The Journalist Premier League (JPL) is an annual cricket tournament celebrating the journalism community and honoring the courage of journalists.

🙏 Tribute Functions

Annual ceremonies honoring departed journalists and distributing financial assistance to their families. Celebrating their legacy and contributions.
