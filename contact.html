Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for welfare support?

Contact us with your details or visit our Programs page to learn about eligibility. We'll guide you through the application process and connect you with available government schemes and trust assistance.

Who is eligible for assistance?

Journalists accredited with the Government of India and those with 5+ years of continuous journalism experience are eligible. We also assist non-accredited journalists. Contact us to verify your eligibility.

How can I participate in training programs?

We organize regular training workshops in digital journalism, safety, legal awareness, and career development. Follow our Events page or contact us to register for upcoming programs.

What is the response time for inquiries?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours. For urgent welfare assistance, please call the contact numbers provided or email directly with "URGENT" in the subject line.

Is my information kept confidential?

Yes, all personal information shared through our contact form is handled with complete confidentiality and used only for addressing your inquiry. We follow strict data protection practices.
